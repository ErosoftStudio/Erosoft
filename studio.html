Erosoft is an independent game studio crafting puzzle and immersive-simulation experiences.

What we believe

Would we want to
play this ourselves?

We build worlds that reward curiosity — systems you can poke, prod, and bend in ways we never planned, and puzzles that make you feel clever for solving them. Every project starts with one question: would we want to play this ourselves? If the answer isn't an emphatic yes, it doesn't ship.

That bar shapes how we work. We prototype fast and cut without mercy. We playtest until the machine hums. We'd rather ship one world that stays with you than three that don't.

✦

Curiosity is the loop

The best moment in our games is a player whispering “wait… what if I…” — and being right.

✦

Simulate, don't script

Real systems produce stories we could never write. We build the machine and let you find the ghost in it.

✦

Respect the player

No hand-holding, no filler. Your time is the most expensive thing in the game.
