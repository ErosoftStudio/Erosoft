The world

The apartment is yours.

Every room is packed with things a dog can interact with — a mini fridge that opens, a bean bag with your name on it, a coffee table at exactly tail height. If it looks like you can mess with it, you probably can.

No waypoints, no fail states. Just a small dog, a big apartment, and an owner who really should have put that soda can somewhere higher.
