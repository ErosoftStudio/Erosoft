01 — The studio

Worlds that reward
curiosity.

Erosoft is an independent studio crafting puzzle and immersive-simulation experiences. Every project starts with one question — would we want to play this ourselves? If the answer isn't an emphatic yes, it doesn't ship.

✦

Systems first

Valves, dials, wiring, light — mechanisms you can poke, prod, and bend in ways we never planned.

✦

Earned cleverness

Puzzles that make you feel smart for solving them. No waypoint arrows, no answer told twice.

✦

Quality bar

Small team, long polish. We ship when it's right, and we play every build ourselves first.
